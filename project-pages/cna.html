Channel News Asia: Apassionato
Documentary | On the Red Dot

Season 1: Episode 37 | Aired December 2016
Apassionato is a short documentary that was produced in collaboration with Channel News Asia (Mediacorp). It was featured as part of their current affairs/info-ed program "On the Red Dot". I primarily served as the director of photography and journalist for this project.
The episode which premiered on 23 December 2016 as part of Episode 37 of Season 1 can be watched on MeWatch. It can also be found in the National Archives of Singapore.
