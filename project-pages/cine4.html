CiNE65 IV: Hello Pa
Director
Short film | CiNE65 IV
Best Screenplay Award
Synopsis: A son gives his father a call one day. However as the conversation develops, the father realises that the call isn't a casual one.

I served as the director of the film and co-wrote the script with five other individuals who worked on the project.
The film went on to win the Best Screenplay award at CiNE65 IV. Hello Pa can be found in the National Archives of Singapore.
