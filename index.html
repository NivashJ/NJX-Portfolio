My Background / About Me
Welcome to my world as a linguist and media practitioner. My journey has been a tapestry of experiences within the dynamic Singapore media landscape, where I've delved into the realms of news, documentary journalism, and photojournalism. With contributions to entities such as Mediacorp and Singapore Press Holdings, my passion for storytelling has taken root, whether through evocative visuals or compelling words.

My academic background as a linguistics graduate has fueled my insatiable curiosity for the intricate workings of language. During my undergraduate studies, I explored various linguistic branches, from psycholinguistics to computational linguistics. Ultimately, I found my calling in sociolinguistics, which became the focus of my final year project.

Beyond academia, my fervor for visual expression led me to captivating worlds of photography, design and filmmaking.
